The gamma distribution is a probability function that describes the wait time until future events.

Probability mass function

\(P_x = \frac{1}{\Gamma(\alpha)\beta ^\alpha}x^{\alpha -1}e^{\frac{-x}{\beta}} \quad x \geq 0\)

\(\Gamma(z) = \int_{0}^{\infty} x^{z-1}e^{-x} \,dx\)
\(\beta \geq 0\ \)
\(\alpha \geq 0\)
