Chi-square

Information

The chi-square distribution is used in the common chi-square tests for goodness of fit, the independence of two criteria of classification of qualitative data, and in confidence interval estimation.

Probability mass function

\(P_x = {n \choose x}p^x(1-p)^{n-x}\)

\(x\) : number of times for a specific outcome within n trials
\(p\) : probability of success on a single trial
\(n\) : number of trials
